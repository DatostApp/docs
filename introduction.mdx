Datost is a Slack-native AI data analyst. Connect your warehouses, CRMs, and product-analytics tools once, then let anyone on your team ask questions in plain English and get governed, sourced answers without leaving Slack.

## What you can do

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Ask in Slack" icon="slack" href="/slack/overview">
    `@Datost` in any channel or DM. Follow up in threads. Get charts, SQL, and explanations inline.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Track metrics" icon="chart-line" href="/capabilities/tracked-metrics">
    Turn any query into a monitored time-series. Datost investigates anomalies and pings Slack.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Schedule research" icon="calendar-clock" href="/capabilities/scheduled-reports">
    Recurring AI research jobs delivered to channels, DMs, or group chats.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Build dashboards" icon="chart-bar" href="/capabilities/dashboards">
    Pin charts from any conversation. Share org-wide or gate by role.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Start here

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Datost in Slack">
    Install the Datost app in your workspace and link your account. See the [Slack install guide](/integrations/communication/slack).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect a data source">
    Pick your warehouse or database — [BigQuery](/integrations/warehouses/bigquery), [Snowflake](/integrations/warehouses/snowflake), [Postgres](/integrations/warehouses/postgresql), and [more](/integrations/warehouses/overview).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask your first question">
    In Slack: `@Datost how many signups did we get last week?` Answers arrive with SQL, chart, and explanation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Datost respects your data's access rules. Users only see data they have permission to see — governed through [custom roles](/workspace/roles-and-permissions).
</Note>
